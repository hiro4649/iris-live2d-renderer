# Best Of N Evidence Policy

Best of N is required only for R3, ambiguous design, migration,
security-sensitive work, large refactors, or architecture tradeoffs. It is not
required for small docs, typo, version marker, or mechanical manifest updates.

Valid evidence includes candidate count, safe summaries of candidates, selected
candidate, reason selected, and reason alternatives were rejected. Raw diffs,
logs, endpoint values, and private paths are forbidden.
