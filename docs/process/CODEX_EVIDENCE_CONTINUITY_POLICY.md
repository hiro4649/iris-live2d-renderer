# Evidence Continuity Policy

Evidence continuity checks that key safe evidence paths survive preflight,
local gate execution, workflow summary generation, final summaries, and safe
artifact indexing. The gate protects product baseline, product verification,
npm diagnostic, evidence pack, human confirmation, complexity oracle,
self-test case export, and score decomposition paths.

The policy does not store raw logs, raw diffs, prompts, endpoint values,
private paths, production data, or personal data.
