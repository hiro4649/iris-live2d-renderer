# Knowledge Governance Policy

AGENTS.md is a map, not a manual. Detailed workflows belong in
`docs/process` and skills. The knowledge map records source-of-record files,
policy indexes, skill indexes, gate indexes, eval suites, review references,
and contract governance references.

The gate verifies that required source-of-record files exist, required policies
and eval suites are indexed, markers match the current harness version, and
AGENTS.md remains compact enough to serve as navigation rather than a long
task-specific playbook.

It does not assert domain truth for downstream repositories and does not reach
the network.
